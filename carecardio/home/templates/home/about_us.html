Why choos Health Care with
CareCardio

CareCardio is dedicated to combating cardiovascular disease (CVD), a significant contributor to premature mortality in the UK. Through our specialised cardiac primary care services, CareCardio focuses on early detection and treatment, enabling patients to live healthier and longer lives.

By facilitating access to genetic testing, implementing preventive treatments, fostering multi-disciplinary collaboration, and promoting public awareness and education, CareCardio strives to save lives, enhance outcomes, and reduce hospital readmissions for individuals affected by CVD. Furthermore, CareCardio emphasizes the importance of improving access to cardiac rehabilitation services, providing essential support for patients on their path to recovery.
